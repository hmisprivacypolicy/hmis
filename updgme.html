Privacy Policy
  • The Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) is committed to protecting your privacy while delivering services through the e-Sushrut UP HMIS (DGME) Mobile App. This privacy statement governs your use of the e-Sushrut UP HMIS (DGME) Mobile App.
  • This app provides services for patients and doctors of UPDGME hospitals that use the Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) developed by the Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC), Noida.

COLLECTION AND USE OF YOUR INFORMATION

  • The application may access or process certain information required for healthcare services including patient identification details, appointment information, and health related information required for hospital consultation and treatment services.
  • Health data such as medical consultation details, prescriptions, and teleconsultation information may be accessed or processed only for the purpose of providing healthcare services through the HMIS platform.
  • The application uses device permissions such as camera and microphone only to enable teleconsultation video calling between doctors and patients. These permissions can be revoked anytime from the device settings.
  • The application does not sell or use user health information for advertising or marketing purposes.

SECURITY OF YOUR INFORMATION

  • CDAC takes appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
  • User data is transmitted securely to authorized hospital systems through the HMIS infrastructure and is accessible only to authorized healthcare providers and hospital administrators.

DATA RETENTION

  • User and health related information may be stored as part of the hospital medical records maintained under the HMIS platform.
  • Data is retained only for as long as required for providing healthcare services, maintaining medical records, and complying with applicable healthcare regulations and government policies.

DATA DELETION

  • Users may request deletion of their personal data by contacting the hospital administration or by sending a request to the official support email listed below.
  • Upon receiving a valid request and verification, the data will be deleted or anonymized in accordance with applicable regulations and hospital record policies.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

  • This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in services or legal requirements. When updates are made, the revised version will be published with the updated date.
