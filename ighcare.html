Privacy Policy

  • The Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) is committed to protecting your privacy, while delivering the software that brings you the features provided in the IGHCare Mobile App. This privacy statement governs your use of the IGHCare Mobile App.
  • This app provides features for patients and doctors of SAIL Rourkela Hospitals which use the Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) of the Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC), Noida.
Collection and Use of Your Information
  • The IGHCare Mobile App does not collect or transmit to CDAC personally identifiable information or require the use of logins, passwords, etc. generated in the mobile app. The app uses the camera and record audio permissions to enable video calling between patients and doctors during teleconsultation workflows. The option to revoke these permissions can be set from the app settings options on the mobile device.
Security of Your Information
  • CDAC respects and protects the privacy of the individuals who use HMIS Mobile App services and is committed to helping protect the security of your information. The information captured by the app is not shared with any third party.
Changes to this Privacy Statement
  • We will occasionally update this privacy statement to reflect changes in our products services and customer feedback. When we post changes, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this statement. We encourage you to periodically review this statement to be informed of how CDAC is protecting your information.
