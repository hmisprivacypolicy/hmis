Privacy Policy

The Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) is committed to protecting your privacy while delivering the software that brings you the features provided in the implementation of integrated HMIS in Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education & Research (JIPMER) and associated health facilities.

This privacy statement governs your use of the C-DAC's HMIS Mobile App for the said project. It does not apply to other online or offline sites, services, or apps by JIPMER.

Collection and Use of Your Information

The HMIS Mobile App does not collect or transmit personally identifiable information or require logins, passwords, etc. The app uses camera and record audio permissions to enable video calling between patients and doctors during tele-consultation workflows & self-visit stamping. File system access permission is used to save and view prescriptions/documents. These permissions can be revoked from the app settings.

Security of Your Information

CDAC respects and protects the privacy of individuals who use HMIS Mobile App services and is committed to protecting the security of your information. The information captured by the app is not shared with any third party.

Changes to this Privacy Statement

We will occasionally update this privacy statement to reflect changes in our products, services, and customer feedback. When we post changes, we will revise the "last updated" date. We encourage you to periodically review this statement to stay informed about how CDAC is protecting your information.
