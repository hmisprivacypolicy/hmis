Railways HMIS Mobile App


  • The Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) is committed to protecting your privacy, while delivering the software that brings you the features provided in the Railways HMIS Mobile App. This privacy statement governs your use of the Railways HMIS Mobile App. It does not apply to other online or offline Railtel, Indian Railways or other sites, services, or apps..
  • This app provides features for patients and doctors of Railway Hospitals which use the Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) of the Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC), Noida.
Collection and Use of Your Information
  • The Railways HMIS Mobile App does not collect or transmit to CDAC personally identifiable information or require the use of logins, passwords, etc. generatedin the mobile app. The app uses the camera and record audio permissions to enable video calling between patients and doctors during teleconsultation workflows. The filesystem access permission is used to save and view prescriptions. The option to revoke these permissions can be set from the app settings options on the mobile device.
Security of Your Information
  • CDAC respets and protects the privacy of the individuals who use Railways HMIS App services and is committed to helping protect the security of your information. The information captured by the app is not shared with any third party
